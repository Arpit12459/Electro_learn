🛩️ Why Learn Aeromodelling?

Aeromodelling combines physics, electronics, programming, and engineering design. It teaches students about aerodynamics, control systems, battery management, and real-time feedback — skills that directly apply to aerospace, robotics, and advanced engineering careers.
